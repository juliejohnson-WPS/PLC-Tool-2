Becoming While Doing

A Cycle of Inquiry Reflection Tool for Learning Leaders & Organizations

Every learning organization begins with context. Identify where you are leading change.

As a learning leader, frame an Essential Question centered around a critical Problem of Practice. What dynamic puzzle are you trying to solve to create hope for your students?

Prompt 1 of 6: Pinpoint the core standards, target skills, or deep conceptual understandings you are focusing on during this cycle.

Prompt 2 of 6: Beyond the academic standard, what dispositions or habits of mind are students "becoming" while doing this specific work?

Prompt 3 of 6: What evidence—both formative indicators and summative milestones—will prove that students have achieved mastery?

Prompt 4 of 6: Design a systemic, supportive response. How will you intervene dynamically to create hope and pathway alternatives for those struggling?

Prompt 5 of 6: How will you extend the learning boundaries? What opportunities will you provide to deepen their expertise and critical thinking?

Prompt 6 of 6: As a collective PLC of learning leaders, what must we learn or change in our instructional stance during this process? How are we 'becoming while doing'?
